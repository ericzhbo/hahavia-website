About Us

27 YEARS OF
PRECISION CRAFT

Zhejiang Sunnic Group Co., Ltd., part of Xinda Group, was founded in 1997. We specialize in the production and trade of premium woven fabrics and high-quality garments.

Our collection includes high-quality cashmere, wool, and nylon yarns, fabrics, and garments; down and padded clothing and bedding sets; knitted and woven garments; furs, leather clothing, and raw materials.

Exporting to the United States, Europe, Southeast Asia, Canada, and more than 30 countries worldwide. We partner with renowned international brands for OEM production.
